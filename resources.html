Did You Know

  • The largest Hispanic country in the world is Brazil, which is the fifth largest country in the world by both land area and population.
  • The highest capital city in the world is La Paz, Bolivia, which is located at an altitude of over 3,800 meters (12,500 feet) above sea level.
  • The largest lake in South America is Lake Titicaca, which is located on the border of Peru and Bolivia and is also the highest navigable lake in the world at an altitude of 3,812 meters (12,507 feet) above sea level.
  • The largest waterfall in the world is the Angel Falls in Venezuela, which has a total height of 979 meters (3,212 feet) and is the highest uninterrupted waterfall in the world.
  • The only country in South America with no indigenous tribes is Chile.
