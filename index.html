Did You Know

  1. Spanish is the second most spoken language in the world
  2. Spanish is an official language in 20 countries, including Spain, Mexico, and most countries in Central and South America
  3. Spanish is a Romance language, which means it evolved from Latin. It is related to other Romance languages such as French, Italian, and Portuguese.

Cultural Background

  1. Spain
    Spain is located in southwestern Europe and is the fourth largest country in Europe. It is known for its beaches, cultural heritage, and lively nightlife. Some famous landmarks in Spain include the Alhambra in Granada, the Sagrada Familia in Barcelona, and the Plaza Mayor in Madrid. Spanish cuisine is known for dishes such as paella, tapas, and sangria.
  2. Mexico
    Mexico is located in North America and is the 14th largest country in the world by land area. It is known for its ancient civilizations, such as the Maya and the Aztec, and its diverse landscapes, including beaches, deserts, and mountains. Some famous landmarks in Mexico include the Pyramid of the Sun in Teotihuacán, the Palacio de Bellas Artes in Mexico City, and the Cenote Ik-Kil in Yucatán. Mexican cuisine is known for dishes such as tacos, burritos, and mole.
  3. Argentina
    Argentina is located in South America and is the second largest country in South America. It is known for its tango music and dance, as well as its stunning landscapes, including the Andes Mountains, the Pampas grasslands, and the Iguazú Falls. Some famous landmarks in Argentina include the Iguazú Falls, the Recoleta Cemetery in Buenos Aires, and the Perito Moreno Glacier in Patagonia. Argentine cuisine is known for dishes such as empanadas, asado (grilled meat), and dulce de leche (sweet milk caramel).
